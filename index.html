Names generator

This names generator was made by Airapport for Conquicktory strategy game. Look what countries names it produces
It's work is based on Markov's chains. The generator defines the probalilities of symbols combinations in the samples, and generates new names using the rules found.
Feel free to play with the generator and generate some names for your RPG, strategy or other game. Here's what you should do for this:
  1. Paste learning samples to the"samples" field (you can the buse some sample samples :) )
  2. Press"Generate" button
  3. Get a list of generated names and use them as you like :)
You can also integrate the whole class MarkovGenerator from the generator.js into your own game. If you want to do this, please, mention Airapport in your game's Credits.
